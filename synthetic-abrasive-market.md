<p><ul><li>Bonded Abrasive</li><li>Coated Abrasive</li><li>Super Abrasive</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The synthetic abrasive market comprises three main types: bonded abrasives, coated abrasives, and super abrasives. Bonded abrasives, like grinding wheels, feature abrasives fixed in a matrix for durability during grinding and cutting. Coated abrasives consist of abrasive grains attached to a backing material, used for sanding and polishing. Super abrasives, such as diamond and cubic boron nitride, are designed for high-performance applications, offering superior hardness and thermal stability for cutting and grinding hard materials.</p></p>
