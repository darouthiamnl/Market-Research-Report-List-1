<p><strong>The Automotive Air Brake System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Air Disc</li><li>Air Drum</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive air brake system market is primarily divided into two types: air disc and air drum brakes. Air disc brakes utilize a rotor and caliper arrangement, offering superior performance and quicker heat dissipation, making them ideal for heavy-duty vehicles. In contrast, air drum brakes feature a drum and shoe system, providing reliable braking but with slower cooling rates. Both systems play a critical role in ensuring vehicle safety, efficiency, and optimal stopping power across various automotive applications.</p></p>
