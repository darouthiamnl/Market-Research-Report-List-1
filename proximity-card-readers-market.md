<p><p>The proximity card reader market encompasses various types, including stand-alone proximity readers and wall switch proximity readers. Stand-alone proximity readers operate independently, allowing users to gain access by simply presenting their cards without needing a connection to a central system, making them ideal for smaller applications. In contrast, wall switch proximity readers are integrated into existing electrical systems, providing a seamless method for accessing secured areas, often combining lighting controls with access management, enhancing convenience and security in commercial settings.</p></p>
