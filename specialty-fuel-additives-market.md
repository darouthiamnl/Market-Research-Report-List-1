<p><p>The specialty fuel additives market comprises several types that enhance fuel performance. Deposit control additives reduce engine deposits, improving efficiency and longevity. Cetane improvers enhance ignition quality in diesel fuels, leading to better combustion. Antioxidants prevent fuel degradation during storage, maintaining stability. Lubricity improvers enhance the lubrication properties of fuels, protecting engine components from wear. Other market segments may include agents for corrosion inhibition, cold flow improvement, and emissions reduction, each contributing to optimized fuel performance and environmental compliance.</p></p>
