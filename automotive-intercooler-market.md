<p><strong>The Automotive Intercooler Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Air-to-Air</li><li>Air-to-Water</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive intercooler market primarily consists of two types: air-to-air and air-to-water intercoolers. Air-to-air intercoolers use ambient air to cool the compressed charge, making them simpler and lighter, thus enhancing efficiency in most applications. In contrast, air-to-water intercoolers employ a water cooling system, allowing for more effective temperature management, especially in high-performance vehicles. Each type offers distinct benefits, with air-to-air being more prevalent in standard cars and air-to-water being favored in racing and high-performance applications.</p></p>
