<p><strong>The Coffee Grounds Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Natural fermentation (dry composting method)</li><li>Decomposition fermentation (wet composting method)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The coffee grounds market can be segmented into two main types: natural fermentation and decomposition fermentation. Natural fermentation, or dry composting, involves spreading coffee grounds in a dry environment, allowing microorganisms to break them down naturally over time. In contrast, decomposition fermentation, or wet composting, utilizes moisture to accelerate the breakdown process, often involving additional organic materials. Both methods serve as sustainable practices for recycling coffee waste, enhancing soil quality, and promoting eco-friendly gardening approaches.</p></p>
