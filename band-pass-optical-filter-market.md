<p><p>The band pass optical filter market is segmented into single-band and multiband categories. Single-band filters transmit light within a specific wavelength range while blocking all others, making them ideal for applications requiring precise wavelength selection. Multiband filters, on the other hand, allow multiple wavelengths to pass through simultaneously, catering to applications that require the detection or manipulation of various wavelengths at once, such as in spectroscopy or telecommunications. Both types play crucial roles in enhancing optical system performance.</p></p>
