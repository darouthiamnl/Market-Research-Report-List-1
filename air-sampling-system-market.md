<p><p>The air sampling system market comprises two primary types: pipe detectors and smoke detectors. Pipe detectors utilize a network of pipes to continuously sample air for particulates, providing early detection of fire, smoke, or hazardous gases. Conversely, smoke detectors are standalone units that sense smoke through optical or ionization methods, triggering alarms to alert occupants. Both systems enhance safety in residential and commercial spaces by providing timely alerts to potential threats, ultimately contributing to fire prevention and improved indoor air quality.</p></p>
