<p><p>The wet shaver market is categorized into three main types: manual shavers, rotary shavers, and reciprocating shavers. Manual shavers, often safety razors or straight razors, offer control and precision, appealing to traditionalists. Rotary shavers use circular blades for a comfortable shave, ideal for contouring and handling longer facial hair. Reciprocating shavers feature blades that move back and forth, providing a quick and efficient shaving experience. Each type caters to different consumer preferences for convenience, comfort, and shaving style.</p></p>
