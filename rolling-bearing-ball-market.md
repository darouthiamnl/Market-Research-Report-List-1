Ceramic balls are known for their high hardness, low weight, and resistance to corrosion, making them ideal for high-speed applications. Plastic balls offer lightweight properties and good resistance to chemicals, suitable for environments where metal would corrode. Steel balls are the most commonly used due to their durability and strength, widely utilized in various industrial applications. Each type caters to specific performance and environmental needs.</p></p>
