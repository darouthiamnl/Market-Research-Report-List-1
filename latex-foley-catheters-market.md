<p><p>Latex Foley catheters are categorized into three main types based on their functionality. Two-way Foley catheters, most commonly used, feature one lumen for urine drainage and another for balloon inflation. Three-way Foley catheters have an additional lumen for continuous bladder irrigation, ideal for post-operative care or managing hemorrhagic conditions. Four-way Foley catheters provide further versatility, including features for bladder irrigation, drainage, and medication delivery, catering to specialized medical needs and improving patient management in urology.</p></p>
