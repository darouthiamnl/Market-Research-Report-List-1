<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Passenger Vehicle Starter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Direct Control</li><li>Electromagnetic Control</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The passenger vehicle starter market is categorized into Direct Control and Electromagnetic Control types. Direct Control systems utilize mechanical links between the ignition switch and the starter, ensuring simpler, more reliable operation. In contrast, Electromagnetic Control systems use solenoids to initiate the starter motor, allowing for more efficient and remote activation. Both types cater to different vehicle requirements, with Direct Control favored for its simplicity, while Electromagnetic Control appeals to modern vehicles requiring advanced automation and integration.</p></p>
