<p><p>The Home Respiratory Monitor market comprises several types of devices designed for monitoring respiratory health. Pulse oximeters measure blood oxygen saturation and pulse rate, providing critical data for patients with respiratory conditions. Incentive spirometers encourage deep breathing exercises to improve lung function and prevent complications. Peak flow meters assess airway obstruction by measuring the maximum speed of expiration, aiding in asthma management. Additionally, the "Other" category includes various devices tailored for specific respiratory needs, enhancing patient care at home.</p></p>
