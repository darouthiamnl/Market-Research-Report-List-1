<p><p>Solar Electric Propulsion (SEP) utilizes solar energy to generate thrust, primarily for space missions. The Hall Effect Thruster (HET) is a popular type, featuring efficiency and high thrust-to-weight ratios ideal for long-duration missions. The Pulsed Plasma Thruster (PPT) offers simplicity and low power requirements, making it suitable for smaller satellites. Other emerging technologies include ion thrusters and arcjet engines, each offering unique advantages in propulsion efficiency and operational capabilities, catering to a diverse array of space applications.</p></p>
