<p><p>Polyoxymethylene (POM) is a high-performance thermoplastic known for its excellent mechanical properties and dimensional stability. The POM market is segmented into two main types: homo-polyoxymethylene and co-polyoxymethylene. Homo-polyoxymethylene features a high degree of crystallinity, offering superior strength and stiffness, making it ideal for precision parts. Co-polyoxymethylene, on the other hand, combines different polymer structures, enhancing flexibility and chemical resistance, and is suited for applications requiring more durability and impact resistance. Both types cater to diverse industrial needs.</p></p>
