<p><p>Metal Oxide Sensors (MOS) are electronic devices that detect gases or volatile compounds through their electrical resistance changes. These sensors utilize metal oxides, such as tin oxide, which react with gas molecules, altering conductivity and enabling detection. Their applications span industries including automotive, environmental monitoring, industrial safety, and healthcare, where they are pivotal for air quality monitoring and detecting hazardous gases.</p><p>The Metal Oxide Sensor Market is anticipated to experience significant expansion, projected to grow at a CAGR of 12.7% during the forecast period. Additionally, the automotive sector's shift towards improving emissions and fuel efficiency is fueling the adoption of MOS for air fuel ratio management. Innovations in material science, leading to more sensitive and selective sensors, are also shaping market dynamics.
