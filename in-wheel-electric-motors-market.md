<p><p>The in-wheel electric motors market features two primary types: outer rotor and inner rotor designs. Outer rotor in-wheel motors have the rotor positioned outside the stator, offering advantages like higher torque density and improved cooling, making them ideal for high-performance vehicles. In contrast, inner rotor in-wheel motors place the rotor inside the stator, providing a more compact design that enhances vehicle aesthetics and allows for easier integration with traditional wheel assemblies. Both types contribute to the growth of electric vehicle technology.</p></p>
