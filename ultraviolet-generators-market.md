<p><strong>The Ultraviolet Generators Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Inline UV Generator</li><li>Open Channel UV Generator</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Ultraviolet Generators market encompasses two primary types: Inline UV Generators and Open Channel UV Generators. Inline UV Generators are designed for direct integration into water or wastewater treatment systems, effectively disinfecting fluids as they flow through. In contrast, Open Channel UV Generators operate in open water channels, exposing large volumes of water to UV light for treatment. Both types are essential for ensuring effective disinfection, preventing harmful pathogens, and meeting regulatory standards for water quality.</p></p>
