<p><p>The silicone market comprises several key types: elastomers, fluids and emulsions, resins, and gels. Elastomers are flexible materials used in diverse applications like seals and automotive parts. Fluids and emulsions serve as lubricants and anti-foaming agents in various industries. Resins are solid or semi-solid substances utilized in adhesives and coatings for their durability. Gels, often used in personal care and medical products, provide a unique texture and consistency for applications requiring viscosity and stability.</p></p>
