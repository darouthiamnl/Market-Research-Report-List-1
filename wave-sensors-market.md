<p><p>The wave sensors market is categorized into three main types: seabed, sea surface, and others. Seabed sensors are deployed on the ocean floor to measure wave dynamics and underwater conditions, providing critical data for research and engineering. Sea surface sensors, typically floating devices, capture wave height, frequency, and direction, essential for marine navigation and weather forecasting. The "others" category encompasses innovative sensor technologies, including airborne systems and remote sensing techniques, broadening the scope of oceanographic and environmental monitoring applications.</p></p>
