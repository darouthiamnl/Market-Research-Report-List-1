<p><p>The industrial part washer market is primarily divided into solvent-based and water/aqueous-based cleaning technologies. Solvent-based washers utilize organic solvents to dissolve grease, oils, and other contaminants, making them effective for precision cleaning but raising environmental and health concerns. In contrast, water/aqueous-based washers employ water mixed with detergents or chemicals for cleaning, offering a more environmentally friendly solution. This type is often preferred for its lower toxicity and ease of disposal, catering to industries focused on sustainability.</p></p>
