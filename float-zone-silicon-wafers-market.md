<p><strong>The Float Zone Silicon Wafers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>P-Type</li><li>N-Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Float Zone (FZ) silicon wafers are categorized into two main types: P-Type and N-Type. P-Type wafers are doped with elements like Boron, creating holes that facilitate positive charge carriers, making them ideal for certain semiconductor applications. In contrast, N-Type wafers are doped with elements such as Phosphorus, introducing extra electrons that act as negative charge carriers. Both types are crucial for diverse applications in electronics and photovoltaics, each offering specific electrical properties tailored for distinct devices.</p></p>
