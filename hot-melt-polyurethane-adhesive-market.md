<p><p>The Hot Melt Polyurethane Adhesive market is categorized into two main types: Non-Reactive and Reactive Hot Melt Adhesives. Non-Reactive Hot Melt Adhesives are thermoplastic in nature, providing quick bonding and ease of application, but with limited resistance to moisture and heat over time. Reactive Hot Melt Adhesives, on the other hand, undergo a chemical reaction upon exposure to moisture, enhancing durability and adhesion properties, making them suitable for demanding applications in industries like automotive, construction, and packaging.</p></p>
