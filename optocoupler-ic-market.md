<p><p>An optocoupler IC, also known as an opto-isolator, is a crucial electronic component that uses light to transfer electrical signals between isolated circuits. This isolation helps to prevent high voltages from affecting the lower voltage side of a system, enhancing safety and signal integrity. Optocouplers are widely used in applications such as power supplies, automotive electronics, and communication systems.</p><p>The Optocoupler IC Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 8.10% during the forecast period.
