<p><p>The recycled carbon fiber market encompasses various forms, including carbon fiber nonwoven mats, chopped carbon fiber, and milled carbon fiber. Carbon fiber nonwoven mats are produced by arranging short fibers into a mat, offering lightweight properties for applications in automotive and aerospace industries. Chopped carbon fiber consists of shorter fiber lengths, ideal for reinforcing plastics and composites. Milled carbon fiber, derived from grinding carbon fiber scraps, provides finer particles for enhanced reinforcement in various products, promoting sustainability and reducing waste.</p></p>
