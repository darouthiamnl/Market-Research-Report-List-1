<p><strong>The Furniture Adhesives Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Solvent-Based</li><li>Water-Based</li><li>Solventless</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Furniture adhesives are categorized into three main types: solvent-based, water-based, and solventless. Solvent-based adhesives contain organic solvents that allow for strong bonds and quick curing but have higher VOC emissions. Water-based adhesives use water as a solvent, offering lower toxicity and easier cleanup but may have longer drying times. Solventless adhesives, often polyurethane or epoxy-based, provide strong bonding capabilities without solvents, resulting in lower environmental impact and better safety profiles, making them ideal for various furniture applications.</p></p>
