<p><p>The Ocean Trenching Device market is primarily divided into two types: Mechanical Type and Jet Type. </p><p>Mechanical Type devices utilize mechanical systems, such as cutters or plows, to excavate and trench the ocean floor, ensuring precise control and stability during operation. In contrast, Jet Type devices employ high-pressure water jets to fluidize the sediment, making it easier to remove material. Both types cater to various applications, including underwater cable installation and resource exploration, enhancing efficiency in maritime operations while minimizing environmental impact.</p></p>
